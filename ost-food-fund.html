I built Our Sunday Table because food gave me something I desperately needed — a way to disappear, to explore, to find joy in the middle of exhaustion. But I have spent my career in global health. I know what food insecurity looks like. I cannot run a food brand and pretend that isn't true.

The Food Fund is not an afterthought. It is the reason this table exists at all. 30% of every product sold here goes directly to vetted food security organisations around the world — not at the end of the year, not when we hit a target. From the very first sale.

And you help govern it. Every quarter, this community votes on where the money goes. Because the people who cook at this table should have a say in who gets fed.

Most brands talk about giving back when they're big enough. Our Sunday Table does it from the beginning — because the point of building something is not to wait until you can afford to care. The business grows only as fast as joy allows. And joy requires feeding people.
