Every Sunday,
something arrives.

I bake at 5am before the house wakes up. By the time Sunday officially begins, there is already something in the oven, something to taste, something to share. When you join this table, it arrives in your inbox too.

This is not a newsletter. It is a Sunday ritual delivered — the recipe, the science behind it, the heritage that inspired it, and the behind-the-scenes of a kitchen that belongs to an executive who needs one day that is entirely her own.

What you get
☀

The Sunday Recipe — every week

Every Sunday, one recipe with the full science breakdown and heritage story. In your inbox before the world wakes up.

⬡

Early access before it publishes

Table members receive the recipe 24 hours before it goes live on the site. You bake it first.

◈

First to know about shop drops

The Ankara Adire Aprons, the Heritage Shortbread Boxes, new products — table members hear first, always.

○

Food Fund voting rights

Every quarter, table members vote on which food security organisations receive the fund. You help govern where the money goes.

◉

Behind the scenes — Sunday mornings

The real 5am kitchen. The mistakes, the experiments, the toddler who wanders in. Table members get the version the feed doesn't show.
